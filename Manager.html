RECENT CLUB'S MANAGER

Sir Alexander Chapman "Alex" Ferguson is a Scottish football coach. He served as head coach of Manchester United before ending his career. Ferguson has won more championships than any other coach in English football history.
David William Moyes is a Scottish football manager and former footballer. Since December 2019, he has served as head coach at West Ham United. His game philosophy requires players to have a solid fitness base.
Louis van Gaal is a Dutch football coach and former midfielder. Since August 2021, he has been the head coach of the Dutch national team
José Mário dos Santos Mourinho Félix is ​​a Portuguese football coach and former player. Mourinho is widely regarded as one of the best football coaches of the 21st century. He led Porto in consecutive years to win the UEFA Cup and the Champions League.
Michael Carrick is an English footballer, a midfielder. Carrick began his professional career at West Ham United and made his debut on the club's representative team in 1999. After playing five seasons at West Ham, Carrick moved to Tottenham Hotspur in 2004-2005
Ole Gunnar Solskjær is a Norwegian football coach and former striker who served as head coach of Manchester United, who play in the English Premier League until 2021.
Ralf Rangnick is a German football coach who coaches Manchester United in the English Premier League. He is a former midfielder.
Erik ten Hag is a Dutch football coach and former Central Defender. Since January 2018, he has served as AFC Ajax head coach.
